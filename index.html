Teleporter

This is my Final Year Project and it takes 4 months to be done by individually. It is a 3D action-adventure game which developed by Unity3D and the programming language used is C#. Player needs to control the game character to move in the maze and use the magical orb to teleport in the game. The gameplay is that player needs to avoid the traps in the game and find the key or solve the puzzle to unlock the portal to escape from the maze to next level. Each level has increase the challenge by adding new traps, more keys require to unlock the portal and more types of puzzles.

Malaysian Bro

It is a 2D platformer game which developed by Unity3D and the programming language used is C#. The game has 4 levels. The player has lives, health and able to use weapon in the game. Each level has increase the challenge by adding new enemies and new layout of the game world. For the first 3 levels, there is a small boss that player needs to defeat it to unlock the door which can go to the next level. The objective of the game is to pass every level until level 4 and beat the boss to win the game.

London Cabbie

This game is my first prototype which made in NightMorning Studios. This is a memory-based game that players need to remember the paths of the blue objects. All objects will start to move when the level is started and players need to redraw the path after the objects are stop moving. All the movement of the objects in the game are random. London Cabbie prototype has 3 levels and the difficulties between the level are depends on the number of red and blue objects in the level and the moving speed of the objects.

Example of screenshot of level 1. Player needs to track the movement of blue object and the red object is to distract the attention of the player.

Faveo

Faveo is an endless color mixing game. Player needs to tap left and right of the screen to hold colors to mix them to match the objective. For example, if the objective is purple color, so player needs to hold blue and red color to mix them into purple to get score. There is health system in the game. If player mismatch the objective the health will be dropped and when the health is zero player will lose the game. The difficulty of the game is based on the falling speed of the color notes but there is a max falling speed.

Infinitree

Infinitree is an idle or clicker game that rely on imagination. Player needs to select a seed and tap to generate them. Players can brew an alcohol once they have enough seeds for the alcohol. Player can also sell the alcohol to customer to get coins and reputation points. Coins can be used to hire a manager to manage the Backyard, Home or Liquer Store. Every manager has different abilities or skills. Reputation points are used to ascend a faction and player can get bonus from the faction after ascend.

Nez: See Everything

Nez: See Everything and Uba: Catchtopia are memory games that player needs to track colored objects while they are moving and find out the colored objects when they are stop moving. Both the games have same gameplay, the only different is the art style of the game. Nez: See Everything has minimalist design that targeted Western market while Uba: Catchtopia which has cute factor of design that targeted Eastern market, kids and women.

My job scope in the games are create a simple particle effect for the correct pick and provide opinions to improve the gameplay. I am also be a playtester to check whether the games have bug before the games are released to the store. Nez: See Everything and Uba: Catchtopia are already available in the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

Rocket Time

Rocket Time is an endless tapping game that player needs to tap on the screen to make the character Uba to jump. However, the screenshots are not from the prototype as it already in a main development. Player needs to tap on a right timing and else the Uba will fall down and player needs to restart from beginning. The game has power-ups that let Uba get higher jump, double coins, and rocket to boost for a certain distance.

The power-ups in the game are random. Player needs to collect the fruits in order to fill up the power-up bar to activate the power-up. After the power-up has been activated then next power-up will be randomly picked for the player. The difficulty of the game is based on the gravity. The further the distance the Uba has travelled, the higher the gravity that apply to Uba.
